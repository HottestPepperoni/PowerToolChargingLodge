# PowerToolChargingLodge
PowerToolChargingLodge – The smart home for your power tool batteries. Organizes, monitors, and charges automatically so your tools are always ready. Comfort for batteries, efficiency for you – welcome to the ultimate charging retreat!

The **PowerToolChargingLodge** is an intelligent charging and management system for cordless power tool batteries. Designed for makers and DIY enthusiasts, it combines organized storage with smart automation to keep your workshop running efficiently.

---

## **What is it?**
Instead of simple wall mounts, this system offers:
- **Dedicated battery slots** with integrated electrical contacts.
- An **ESP32-based control unit** that monitors battery health and charge levels.
- **Automatic charging logic** using a central charger, controlled via high-current relays and galvanically isolated circuits.
- **Safety-first design**: No relay switching under load, verified connections before power-up.

---

## **Key Features**
- **Cyclic charge state measurement/monitoring**.
- **Configurable thresholds** for automatic charging.
- **Sequential charging** on demand.
- **Logging and future expansion** (e.g., web interface, notifications).
- **Universal concept**: Easily adaptable for different brands and battery systems by using manufacturer-specific chargers and interchangeable adapter plates.

---

## **Why?**
Keep your batteries organized, charged, and ready without manual intervention. No more guessing which pack is full or swapping chargers endlessly—your tools stay powered, and your workflow stays smooth.
